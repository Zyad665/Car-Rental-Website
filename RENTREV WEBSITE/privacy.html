Privacy Policy

At RENTREV, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • Driver's license and payment details
  • Vehicle preferences and rental history
  • Device and usage data (IP address, browser type)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Process rentals and payments
  • Improve our services and fleet
  • Send booking confirmations and updates
  • Prevent fraud and ensure safety

3. Data Sharing

We only share your information with:

  • Insurance providers (for rental coverage)
  • Payment processors to complete transactions
  • Law enforcement if required by law

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or delete your personal data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information

5. Security Measures

We protect your data through:

  • SSL encryption for all online transactions
  • Secure storage of physical documents
  • Regular security audits

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic
  • Enable online bookings
