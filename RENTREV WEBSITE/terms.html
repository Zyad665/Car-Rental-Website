1. Rental Agreement

The rental agreement begins at the time of vehicle pickup and ends when the vehicle is returned to RENTREV.

  • A security deposit is required for all rentals.
  • Mileage limits apply as specified in your rental agreement.

2. Payment & Fees

All rentals are subject to the following payment terms:

  • Full payment is due at the time of vehicle pickup.
  • Late returns will incur additional charges (1.5x the daily rate).

3. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made:
